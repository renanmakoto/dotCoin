Privacy Policy

Last updated: August 28, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to dotExchange. This app allows users to check real-time and historical exchange rates between multiple currencies, including fiat and cryptocurrencies. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. dotCoin only uses publicly available exchange rate APIs and does not collect or store any personal data.

2. Information We Collect

No Personal Data Collection

dotExchange does not collect, store, or share any personal information. The app only uses the currency codes and conversion requests you provide to fetch exchange rate data from official and public APIs.

3. How We Use Information

Your input (selected currencies and amounts) is used solely to retrieve conversion results and exchange rates. All requests are processed externally by the selected third-party APIs, and no data is stored on our servers.

4. Third-Party Services

Exchange Rate APIs Only

dotExchange uses publicly available APIs, such as:

  • Banco Central do Brasil (PTAX API)
  • European Central Bank (ECB)
  • CoinGecko and other public cryptocurrency APIs

No other third-party services are integrated that collect or process your data.

5. Security

Safe and Local Use

No sensitive data is handled or stored, so the app maintains your privacy by design. All operations are performed securely and locally on your device.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by the "Last updated" date above. We recommend checking this page periodically.
